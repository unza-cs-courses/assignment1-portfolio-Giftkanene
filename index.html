About Me

Hello! I'm Gift Kanene, a passionate Computer Science student majoring in Software Engineering at a university in Lusaka, Zambia. My journey into web development started with curiosity — a simple desire to understand how the websites I visited every day actually worked. That curiosity quickly turned into a deep love for crafting digital experiences.

I specialise in building clean, accessible, and responsive user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I believe great web development is not just about making things look good — it's about making them work seamlessly for every user, on every device. I follow a mobile-first approach and take accessibility seriously in everything I build.

Beyond the front-end, I'm growing my skills in back-end technologies like Node.js and databases, with the goal of becoming a well-rounded full-stack developer. I enjoy collaborative projects, open-source contributions, and tackling real-world problems through technology. When I'm not coding, I'm exploring new frameworks, contributing to study groups, or mentoring peers.
